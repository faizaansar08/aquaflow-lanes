Technical Specifications

Bioswale Design

  • Depth: 0.6-1.2m
  • Width: 1.5-3m
  • Slope: 1-3%
  • Filter media: Sand-gravel mix
  • Underdrain: Perforated pipe

Plant Species

  • Vetiver grass
  • Bamboo species
  • Native shrubs
  • Flowering plants
  • Tree canopy layer

IoT Sensor Suite

  • Water quality meters
  • Flow rate sensors
  • Temperature monitors
  • Soil moisture sensors
  • Air quality monitors

Maintenance Protocol

  • Weekly inspections
  • Monthly cleaning
  • Quarterly sediment removal
  • Seasonal pruning
  • Annual system audit
